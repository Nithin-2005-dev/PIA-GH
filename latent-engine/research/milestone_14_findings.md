# Milestone 14 Findings

## Observation 1

Expertise quantity and expertise coverage are different concepts.

A module may have high expertise while still being vulnerable due to a small contributor pool.

---

## Observation 2

Coverage quality improves with contributor diversity.

Multiple knowledgeable contributors create organizational resilience.

---

## Observation 3

Average expertise alone is insufficient.

Coverage analysis should account for both:

* Expertise strength
* Expertise distribution

The coverage multiplier provides an initial approximation.

---

## Observation 4

Coverage gaps are actionable.

Unlike raw expertise metrics, coverage gaps directly indicate where organizations should invest knowledge transfer efforts.

---

## Observation 5

Coverage ranking provides prioritization.

Organizations can focus attention on the weakest modules first.

---

## Key Insight

Knowledge health depends not only on how much expertise exists, but also on how widely that expertise is distributed.

PIA now identifies modules that are vulnerable due to insufficient expertise coverage.
