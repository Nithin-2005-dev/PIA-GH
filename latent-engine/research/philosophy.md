# Research Notes

## Fundamental Observation

Organizations do not explicitly store concepts such as expertise, ownership, trust, or risk.

They only store events.

Examples:

* commits
* reviews
* issues
* comments

Latent organizational knowledge must therefore be inferred.

---

## Core Pipeline

```
Reality

↓

Event

↓

Evidence

↓

Latent Estimate

↓

Knowledge

↓

Decision
```

---

## Principle 1

Facts are immutable.

Interpretations are derived.

Beliefs evolve.

---

## Principle 2

The system never mutates history.

Instead, new estimates are produced from replaying evidence.

---

## Principle 3

All intelligence emerges from evidence, not directly from events.

This allows multiple latent variables to share a common evidence stream.

Examples:

* Expertise
* Ownership
* Trust
* Burnout
* Leadership
