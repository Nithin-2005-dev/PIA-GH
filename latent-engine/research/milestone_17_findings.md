# Milestone 17 Findings

## Observation 1

Organizations prefer summary metrics.

Individual signals are useful, but decision-makers often need a concise assessment.

---

## Observation 2

Health emerges from multiple dimensions.

Coverage, concentration, and bus factor each contribute different perspectives.

---

## Observation 3

Composite metrics simplify prioritization.

A single score enables ranking and comparison across modules.

---

## Observation 4

Health scoring creates a bridge between operational intelligence and executive reporting.

---

## Observation 5

The health index acts as a foundation for historical analysis and forecasting.

---

## Key Insight

Repository health is not determined by a single signal.

Organizational resilience emerges from the interaction of expertise, ownership, coverage, concentration, and risk.

PIA now provides a unified view of module health.
