# Milestone 18 Findings

## Observation 1

Static health measurements are insufficient.

Organizational health must be analyzed across time.

---

## Observation 2

A module with acceptable health today may still be deteriorating rapidly.

Trend analysis reveals risks before they become critical.

---

## Observation 3

Historical intelligence is fundamentally different from snapshot intelligence.

Understanding change often provides more value than understanding state.

---

## Observation 4

Slope-based trend analysis provides a simple but effective measure of directional health.

---

## Observation 5

Trend ranking enables proactive intervention.

Organizations can prioritize modules showing the fastest decline.

---

## Key Insight

Organizational resilience depends not only on current health but also on the direction of change.

PIA now reasons about health trajectories rather than isolated observations.
