# Milestone 20 Findings

## Observation 1

Prediction and simulation are fundamentally different capabilities.

Forecasting predicts likely futures.

Simulation evaluates alternative futures.

---

## Observation 2

Ownership concentration alone is insufficient to estimate organizational resilience.

Successor readiness significantly reduces departure impact.

---

## Observation 3

Knowledge transfer planning directly influences simulated organizational outcomes.

Training successors creates measurable resilience gains.

---

## Observation 4

Counterfactual reasoning enables proactive organizational decision-making.

Organizations can evaluate risks before events occur.

---

## Observation 5

Simulation benefits from integrating multiple intelligence layers.

Health, ownership, successors, and transfer planning become more valuable when combined.

---

## Key Insight

Organizational resilience is not a static property.

It can be actively improved through intervention, training, and knowledge transfer.

PIA now evaluates not only what is likely to happen, but also what could happen under alternative scenarios.
