# Milestone 15 Findings

## Observation 1

Coverage and concentration are different signals.

A module may have acceptable coverage while still depending heavily on a single contributor.

---

## Observation 2

Knowledge concentration creates hidden organizational risk.

Traditional expertise metrics often fail to detect this condition.

---

## Observation 3

The strongest contributor provides a useful first approximation of concentration.

Even a simple ratio can expose dangerous dependency patterns.

---

## Observation 4

Knowledge distribution is as important as knowledge quantity.

Healthy teams distribute expertise across multiple contributors.

---

## Observation 5

Concentration analysis complements coverage analysis.

Together they provide a more complete understanding of repository health.

---

## Key Insight

The existence of expertise does not guarantee organizational resilience.

Knowledge must be distributed, not merely present.

PIA now identifies modules where expertise concentration creates potential organizational vulnerability.
