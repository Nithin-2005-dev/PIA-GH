# Milestone 13 Findings

## Observation 1

Previous milestones focused primarily on modules.

This milestone introduces a developer-centric perspective.

Questions shift from:

"Who knows this module?"

to:

"What does this developer know?"

---

## Observation 2

Expertise distribution is an important organizational signal.

Two developers may have similar total expertise while covering very different portions of the repository.

Coverage visibility is essential.

---

## Observation 3

Simple aggregation provides meaningful insights.

Grouping expertise estimates by developer immediately reveals:

* Top contributors
* Broad contributors
* Narrow specialists

without introducing complex modeling.

---

## Observation 4

Covered modules improve explainability.

Total expertise alone is insufficient.

Knowing which modules contribute to expertise improves trust and interpretability.

---

## Observation 5

Expertise mapping creates a foundation for coverage analysis.

Future milestones can leverage expertise profiles to identify:

* Knowledge gaps
* Coverage bottlenecks
* Overloaded contributors
* Under-supported modules

---

## Key Insight

Repository expertise is not only a module property.

It is also a developer property.

PIA now supports both perspectives.
