# Milestone 21 Findings

## Observation 1

Analytics alone does not improve organizational health.

Actionable recommendations are required.

---

## Observation 2

Different interventions have different expected impacts.

Prioritization is necessary.

---

## Observation 3

Forecast severity is a strong indicator of intervention urgency.

Modules with severe predicted deterioration require immediate action.

---

## Observation 4

Coverage and concentration represent distinct organizational weaknesses and require different remediation strategies.

---

## Observation 5

Intervention planning creates a bridge between simulation and decision-making.

---

## Key Insight

The value of organizational intelligence is maximized when insights are converted into prioritized actions.

PIA now supports action-oriented decision intelligence rather than purely descriptive or predictive intelligence.
