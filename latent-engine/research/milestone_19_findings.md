# Milestone 19 Findings

## Observation 1

Historical trends can be used to estimate future health.

---

## Observation 2

Future risk is not always visible from current health.

A healthy module may still be deteriorating rapidly.

---

## Observation 3

Absolute deterioration and relative deterioration are different signals.

Both are useful.

---

## Observation 4

Forecasting transforms organizational intelligence from reactive to proactive.

---

## Observation 5

Prediction creates the foundation for simulation.

Simulation requires a model of future system behavior.

---

## Key Insight

Organizations should prioritize modules based on predicted deterioration rather than only current risk.

PIA now reasons about likely future states rather than only observed historical states.
