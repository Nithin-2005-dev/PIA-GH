# Milestone 16 Findings

## Observation 1

Identifying risk is not sufficient.

Organizations require recommended actions in addition to diagnostics.

---

## Observation 2

Knowledge transfer naturally emerges from combining multiple intelligence layers.

Relevant inputs include:

* Ownership
* Successor Planning
* Concentration Analysis

---

## Observation 3

Knowledge transfer planning is the first prescriptive capability in PIA.

Previous milestones focused primarily on observation and assessment.

---

## Observation 4

Prioritization is essential.

Organizations often have more risks than resources.

Transfer activities should therefore be ranked by urgency.

---

## Observation 5

Transfer planning creates a bridge between intelligence and execution.

The value of organizational analytics increases significantly when recommendations are actionable.

---

## Key Insight

Organizational resilience improves when intelligence systems recommend actions rather than only reporting conditions.

PIA now generates prioritized knowledge transfer recommendations.
