# Milestone 9 Findings

## Observation 1

Ownership distributions can be transformed into risk assessments.

Risk emerges from concentration of knowledge rather than raw activity volume.

---

## Observation 2

Bus Factor is a derived organizational signal.

It is not directly observed.

It emerges from:

Activity
→ Expertise
→ Ownership
→ Risk

---

## Observation 3

Coverage thresholds provide flexibility.

Different organizations may define resilience differently.

Examples:

70% coverage

80% coverage

90% coverage

The policy abstraction allows future customization.

---

## Observation 4

Risk levels are easier to reason about than raw bus factor values.

Managers think in:

HIGH

MEDIUM

LOW

rather than:

1

2

3

The RiskLevel abstraction simplifies future decision systems.

---

## Observation 5

Current risk assessment is ownership-based.

Future risk models may additionally consider:

* Expertise decay trends
* Team size
* Review activity
* Documentation quality
* Organizational structure

---

## Key Insight

Risk is an emergent property of knowledge concentration.

PIA now infers organizational resilience directly from observed engineering activity.
