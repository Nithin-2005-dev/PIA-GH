# Milestone 10 Findings

## Observation 1

Risk metrics alone are insufficient.

A bus factor value requires interpretation before it becomes actionable.

KnowledgeRisk introduces contextual explanations.

---

## Observation 2

Risk prioritization is as important as risk detection.

Organizations rarely act on every risk simultaneously.

Ranking allows focus on the most critical modules first.

---

## Observation 3

Policy-driven insight generation remains valuable.

KnowledgeRiskPolicy allows future implementations such as:

* Trend-based risk detection
* Documentation-aware risk detection
* LLM-generated explanations
* Multi-factor risk scoring

without modifying orchestration.

---

## Observation 4

Repository-wide visibility changes decision quality.

Analyzing individual modules is useful.

Ranking all risks provides strategic visibility.

This shifts PIA from analysis to prioritization.

---

## Observation 5

Current knowledge risk assessments are rule-based.

Future assessments may additionally consider:

* Expertise decay velocity
* Review activity
* Documentation quality
* Ownership stability
* Organizational changes

---

## Key Insight

Organizational risk is not merely detected.

Organizational risk must be prioritized.

PIA now performs both.
