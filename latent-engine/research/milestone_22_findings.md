# Milestone 22 Findings

## Observation 1

Intent classification provides a stable abstraction between user questions and organizational capabilities.

---

## Observation 2

Adapter-based integration prevents the reasoning agent from becoming tightly coupled to domain logic.

---

## Observation 3

Intervention Planning and Simulation produce the most valuable organizational responses.

These capabilities combine intelligence from multiple previous milestones.

---

## Observation 4

Natural-language access significantly improves usability compared to direct service execution.

---

## Observation 5

The quality of agent responses depends primarily on the underlying intelligence layers rather than the agent itself.

---

## Key Insight

The agent is not the intelligence.

The agent is the orchestration layer that exposes organizational intelligence built across M1-M21.

The success of M22 is a direct consequence of the depth of the underlying intelligence stack.
